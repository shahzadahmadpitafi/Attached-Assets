5 ESSENTIAL TIPS FOR FIRST-TIME HOME BUYERS

1. SET YOUR BUDGET REALISTICALLY
Know what you can afford including down payment, monthly installments, and additional costs.

2. RESEARCH THE LOCATION
Consider proximity to schools, hospitals, markets, and your workplace. Visit at different times.

3. INSPECT THOROUGHLY
Never skip professional inspection. Check electrical, plumbing, structure, and legal documents.

4. VERIFY LEGAL DOCUMENTS
Ensure clear title, NOC, approved map, and all property documents are authentic.

5. WORK WITH PROFESSIONALS
Hire trusted real estate experts who understand the local market and can guide you properly.
