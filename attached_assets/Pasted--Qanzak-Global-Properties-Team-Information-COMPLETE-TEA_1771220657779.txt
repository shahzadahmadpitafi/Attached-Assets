## ADMIN PANEL - TEAM MANAGEMENT FORM

### Add/Edit Team Member Form Fields:

**Basic Information:**
- Full Name (required)
- Position/Title (required) - e.g., "CEO", "Director of Sales"
- Role Description (required) - e.g., "Chief Executive Officer"
- Department (dropdown)
  - Executive Leadership
  - Sales & Marketing
  - Property Management
  - Client Relations
  - Finance
  - Legal
  - Other
- Specialization (text area) - comma-separated

**Bio & Description:**
- Short Bio (150-200 words) - Rich text editor
- Responsibilities (bullet points) - Add/Remove fields
- Years of Experience (number)
- Education (optional)
- Certifications (optional)

**Contact Information:**
- Phone Number (required)
- Email Address (required)
- WhatsApp Number
- Alternative Contact Number

**Photo:**
- Profile Photo Upload (square format recommended: 800x800px)
- Photo should be professional headshot

**Social Media:**
- LinkedIn URL
- Facebook Profile URL
- Instagram Handle
- Twitter/X Handle

**Display Settings:**
- Featured Team Member (checkbox) - appears on homepage
- Show on Team Page (checkbox)
- Show on Contact Page (checkbox)
- Display Order (number) - for sorting on website
- Active Status (toggle) - show/hide from website

**Statistics (Optional):**
- Properties Sold/Managed (number)
- Years of Experience (number)
- Happy Clients (number)

**Internal Notes:**
- Admin-only notes about this team member
- Not visible on website
