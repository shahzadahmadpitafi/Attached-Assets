### Color Usage:
- Primary backgrounds: White, Light Gray (#f9fafb)
- Accent backgrounds: Navy Blue (#1e40af)
- Highlights: Gold (#d4af37)

- Use high-quality, professional photos

- Show: Modern homes, happy families, professional team, Islamabad/Lahore landmarks

- Always include logo on every material
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Use white logo on dark backgrounds
